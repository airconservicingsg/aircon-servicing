Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) – Aircon Servicing in Singapore

1. How often should I service my aircon in Singapore?

For residential units, service every 3-6 months is recommended. Commercial air conditioners may require monthly maintenance due to higher usage.

2. What happens if I don’t service my aircon regularly?

A poorly maintained aircon can lead to higher electricity bills, weak cooling, water leaks, and frequent breakdowns. Regular servicing prevents costly repairs and improves efficiency.
